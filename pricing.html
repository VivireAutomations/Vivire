FAQ

Common questions

What does the setup fee cover?
The setup fee covers everything needed to get your automations live — discovery sessions, building and configuring each workflow, connecting your tools and platforms, testing, and the official launch. It's a one-time charge paid at the start of the project.
What does the monthly retainer cover?
The monthly retainer covers ongoing maintenance, monitoring, bug fixes when tools update, performance optimisation, and support. As your business grows and changes, we update your automations to keep up — you're never left maintaining them yourself.
Can I upgrade my package later?
Yes. You can start on Starter and upgrade to Growth or Scale at any time. You'll only pay the difference in setup cost for the additional services added, plus the new monthly rate going forward.
How long does setup take?
Starter packages typically go live within 1–2 weeks. Growth packages take 2–3 weeks. Scale packages take 3–5 weeks depending on the complexity of your existing systems and integrations.
Do you offer custom quotes?
Absolutely. If your needs don't fit neatly into one of the packages, book a free strategy call and we'll put together a custom proposal tailored exactly to your business, budget, and goals.
