Our Story

Built for businesses tired of working harder instead of smarter

Vivire Automations was founded with one simple observation: too many talented teams were drowning in manual, repetitive work that had nothing to do with their actual expertise. Emails, data entry, follow-ups, reporting — tasks that consumed hours every day without creating a single dollar of real value.

We set out to change that. By combining deep expertise in AI, workflow design, and business operations, we design automation systems that do the heavy lifting — so your team can focus on the work that actually moves the needle.

Today, Vivire works with businesses across industries — from lean startups to growing enterprises — delivering custom automation that scales with them every step of the way.

What We Stand For

The principles behind everything we build

Automation-First

We default to automation before adding headcount. Every process we touch is evaluated for how it can be streamlined, scaled, or eliminated entirely.

Results-Driven

We don't measure success in deliverables — we measure it in outcomes. Every system we build is tied to a real, trackable business result: time saved, costs cut, revenue generated.

Client-Centered

Your business is unique. We don't sell templates — we design custom solutions built around your exact workflows, goals, and growth stage. Your success is the only metric that matters.
